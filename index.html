How does the 30-day appointment guarantee work?

If we can't secure you a visa appointment within 30 calendar days of signing up, we'll refund your entire payment. Our network and systems allow us to find appointments that aren't typically visible to the public.

Can your service help with visa denials?

While we can't guarantee approval, our system identifies potential red flags in your application and helps you address them before your interview. Our mock interviews also prepare you for tough questions, significantly improving your chances.

How accurate is your AI assistant?

Our AI assistant combines the latest visa regulations with real experiences from thousands of successful applicants. It's continuously updated with the latest information and is trained to provide accurate, personalized guidance.

What types of visas do you support?

We support all major US visa categories including B1/B2 (tourist/business), F1 (student), H1B (work), J1 (exchange), and others. Our system is customized to the specific requirements of each visa type.

How many mock interviews can I do?

Our package includes unlimited mock interviews. Practice as many times as you need until you feel confident. The system will also provide feedback on your responses to help you improve.
